Spaghetti


Eddie Spaghetti

Something quick and easy that never gets old.
Spaghetti an italian classic.

Ingredients


  • Meatballs
  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps to make Spaghetti

  1. Boil spaghetti pasta until cooked.
  2. Pour tomato sauce and mix with pasta.
  3. Cook meat balls and add to spaghetti mix.
  4. Serve with mozzarella cheese :)
