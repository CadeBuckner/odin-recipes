Ingredients


  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • olive oil

Steps to make and bake a pizza.


  1. First off your gonna need to grease your pan with olive oil.
  2. Second you need to put your pizza dough in the pan.
  3. Third your gonna spread the tomato sauce over the dough leaving some space for the crust.
  4. Fourth add your cheese the more the merrier!
  5. Last but not least your gonna add the pepperoni.
